Hung Hoang

About

I finished my doctorate at ETH Zürich, in the Theory of Combinatorial Algorithm Group of Prof. Bernd Gärtner and Prof. Emo Welzl. My supervisor was Prof. Bernd Gärtner. Before, I studied a master's programme in operational research at London School of Economics, where I wrote a thesis under the supervision of Prof. Gregory Sorkin.

My general research interests are algorithms, computational complexity, discrete structure, and optimisation. In particular, I am interested in algorithmic questions in many areas such as combinatorial optimisation, graph theory, operational research, and computational geometry.
